        Announcements

        New Announcements appear directly below the repositionable bar. Reorder by dragging announcements to new positions. Move priority announcements above the repositionable bar to pin them to the top of the list and prevent new announcements from superseding them. The order shown here is the order presented to students. Students do not see the bar and cannot reorder announcements.

        • Access to Course Information

          Item is not available.

          Posted on: Sunday, August 13, 2017 8:36:00 PM MST

          Dear Students!

          Please be informed that the course information for PHY 132 will be introduced on two sites: Labs Master Course Site and your Individual section site.

          The general information about the course (e.g. the syllabus, course schedules, lab reschedule form, lab write-ups, general announcements, etc) will be provided on the Master Site, while specific information regarding your section (e.g.  Prelabs, your grades, your TA’s contact info, etc) will be on the section site.

          Make sure you visit both sites regularly to have complete and up-to-date information about your course.

          The Master Site can be accessed using the link on the Blackboard Home as well as from the link on your section site which will open a new window for the Master Site.


          Posted by: Armen Hakhoyan

        • LAB REPORT FOR FIRST EXPERIMENT

          Item is not available.

          Posted on: Sunday, August 13, 2017 8:41:00 PM MST

          Dear Students,

          The lab report for the first experiment (ONLY) could be a GROUP lab report.
          Each student should submit his or her lab report, which may be exactly the same as the other partners’ lab reports.
          Please work together as a group and share you experience writing lab reports with your partners.
          Starting from the next lab experiment you should submit INDIVIDUAL lab reports.
